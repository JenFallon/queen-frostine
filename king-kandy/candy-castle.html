King Kandy is the king of Candy Land

No adventure is complete without a trip to his Candy Castle.


You'll find the castle below the Ice Cream Mountains where the Chocolate river flows. Made of sparkling sugar, colorful candies and gumdrops galore, Candy Castle is a place you just have to see. King Kandy loves all his sweet Candy Land friends so much, Each year he throws them the Sweet Celebration - the sweetest party you'll ever see!
